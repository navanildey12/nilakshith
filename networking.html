FAQ

Networking Questions Answered

Can you work with our existing network equipment?

Yes. We can work with existing routers, switches, and access points from most major brands. We'll assess your current setup and recommend the most cost-effective path forward.

Do you handle structured cabling?

Yes. We handle Cat5e/Cat6 structured cabling, rack mounting, patch panel termination, and cable management for new and existing installations.

Can you set up VLANs and separate networks for different departments?

Absolutely. VLAN segmentation for departments (admin, production, guest Wi-Fi, CCTV, etc.) is one of our core competencies.

Do you offer an AMC for IT network infrastructure?

Yes. We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) covering regular network health checks, firmware updates, configuration backup, and priority support call-outs.

Do you provide IT support and enterprise Wi-Fi networking solutions outside Silchar?

Yes. Nilakshith Enterprise offers comprehensive IT networking, commercial router configurations, corporate Wi-Fi setups, and structural cabling services across Cachar, Hailakandi, and Karimganj districts. We support offices, schools, and organizations throughout the entire Barak Valley.
