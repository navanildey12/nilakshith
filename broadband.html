FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the installation free?

Yes, installation charges are completely free when you opt for a 6-month initial upfront recharge on any broadband plan. For standard month-to-month plans, standard installation fees apply.

Are the plans unlimited?

Yes. All plans come with unlimited data — no FUP (Fair Use Policy) limits. Stream, download, and work as much as you need.

Are routers provided?

Yes, a high-quality dual-band Wi-Fi router is provided with all our new broadband installations. The device remains the property of Nilakshith Enterprise and is maintained by us at no extra cost. If you prefer to use your own router, our engineers will gladly configure it during setup.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed within 2–4 hours on the same day as the survey, depending on cable length and building structure.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time with a simple call or WhatsApp message. Plan changes typically take effect within 24 hours.

Do you provide support outside business hours?

Our official hours are Mon–Sat, 9 AM to 7 PM. For urgent outages, you can WhatsApp us and we'll assess the situation and respond as quickly as possible.

Which areas in Barak Valley do you provide WiFi and broadband services?

We provide high-speed fiber broadband and WiFi connections across major areas of Barak Valley, including Silchar town (Rangirkhari, Kathal Road, Link Road, Sonai Road, etc.), Karimganj, and Hailakandi. If you are looking for the best WiFi in Silchar, Hailakandi, or Karimganj, our engineers can install FTTH (Fiber to the Home) lines at your home or office on the same day.

Why is Nilakshith Enterprise considered the best WiFi service provider in Barak Valley?

We are consistently rated as one of the best WiFi providers in Silchar, Hailakandi, and Karimganj because of our high-speed fiber network, zero-latency connections, unlimited downloads with no FUP limits, and localized 24/7 customer support. Unlike national ISPs, our local team is always available to resolve line issues within hours.
