FAQ

CCTV Questions Answered

Which camera brands do you install?

We work with leading brands including Hikvision, Dahua, CP Plus, and others. We recommend the best option based on your budget and requirements.

Can I view cameras on my smartphone?

Yes. We set up remote viewing on your Android or iOS phone using the manufacturer's app. You can watch live and recorded footage from anywhere.

How many cameras do I need?

It depends on your property size and coverage needs. Our technician will survey your location and recommend the optimal number and placement of cameras.

Do the cameras record at night?

Yes. All cameras we install have infrared (IR) night vision capability for clear footage even in low-light or complete darkness.

How many days of recording can I store?

It depends on your storage drive capacity and the number/resolution of cameras. A standard 1TB surveillance-grade hard drive typically stores about 15–20 days of continuous recording for 4 HD cameras. We can also configure motion-triggered recording to extend storage duration substantially.

Do you provide a warranty on the equipment?

Yes. We offer a 1-year manufacturer warranty on all major equipment including cameras, DVRs, NVRs, and hard drives. We also back our workmanship with a 30-day installation warranty for any cabling or alignment adjustments.

Where do you offer CCTV camera installation services in Barak Valley?

We provide professional CCTV camera installation and AMC services throughout Silchar, Karimganj, and Hailakandi. Our team specializes in custom camera setup, outdoor security planning, and remote viewing configuration for homes, offices, schools, and retail shops in Barak Valley.
